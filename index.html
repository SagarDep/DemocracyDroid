Watch Democracy Now!

Democracy Now! is an independent daily news broadcast (licensed under creative commons). They refer to themselves as "the War and Peace Report." Democracy Now! is a wonderful alternative to main stream media.

Free Democracy Now!

This application parses the Democracy Now (democracynow.org) rss. There is a non-free democracy Now! application on the google play store, and so I wrote this. Although this has less features, there are some positives: the videos aren't downloaded to the device (and sucking up memory) but streamed through the browser or VLC (I use VLC). Unlike the un-free version, this application doesn't stream audio.</ br> I'm using the SimplisticRss library by ShirwaM, modified to get the video url.
