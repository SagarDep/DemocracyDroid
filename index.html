Free Democracy Now! for Android

This application streams, either in audio or video, the independent media broadcast Democracy Now! The War and Peace Report. Find it on the google PlayStore.

Democracy Now?!?

Democracy Now! is a national, daily, independent, award-winning news program hosted by journalists Amy Goodman and Juan Gonzalez. Pioneering the largest public media collaboration in the U.S.
